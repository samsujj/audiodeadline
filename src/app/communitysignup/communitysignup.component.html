Who should join ArtistXP?

  • Fans of music, modeling and dance
  • Anyone interested in a new social experience
  • Musicians of any Genre
  • DJs and Producers
  • Vocalists and Rappers
  • Models
  • Hip Hop and Video Dancers

WHAT HAPPENS ON ArtistXP is EXCITING.

We have special events and a quarterly live streamed show, The AudioDeadline.com Experience, where musicians, models and dancers win a chance to participate.

Artists along with all the regular users access features you would expect from a complete social experience. This includes friending, inviting others to join, stream chat (like your Facebook wall) and instant messaging. We have several unique features to our platform as well such as play list management, voting opportunities and more.

One of the biggest things that sets us apart and makes ArtistXP a premier destination on the web is the Artist Appreciation and Interactive features. Fans and other artists show what they have liked as well as media they have saved from the Artists on their video and audio media playlists. These are visible to all their friends and gives additional exposure directly to the artists they love.

What are we doing for you as an Artist?

The Excitement

  • Trade traffic in our “Artist Exchange” with other Artists through ArtistXP, Facebook, Twitter, Google+, LinkedIN, Tumblr and soon to be added Instagram. Get massive exposure through trading media!
  • Enter our Artist competitions for a chance to be on our show AudioDeadline.com and other relevant industry opportunities.
  • Get noticed by a steady stream of fans coming watching the AudioDeadline.com Experience live stream and to experience the artist driven social environment.

The Experience

  • Add a full artist profile that is interactive and feature rich built to promote YOU.
  • Upload video content directly or imbed YouTube or Video
  • Add in your SoundCloud player account directly on profile
  • Add your Reverbnation widgets directly to your profile page
  • Add music in several audio formats to your artist player options
  • Add any number of photo galleries to your profile
  • Direct link Facebook page, Instagram and Twitter accounts to stream on your artist page
  • Build a list of fans that visit and like your Artist page
  • Add in Links to any other off site resource such as ModelMayhem, CDBaby, ITunes or anywhere else
